About Al.Khair’s Excellence

Al.Khair.org - Where You Get The Best Hajj and Umrah Services 2024

For over a decade, Al.Khair has been Pakistan’s foremost premium Hajj & Umrah Service Provider. Established in 1990, and incorporated as a private limited in 2005, Al.Khair is the first preference for those who wants to perform Hajj and Umrah with a sense of ease, luxury and comfort.
A team of experienced travel experts manages the Al.Khair portfolio. This includes Local and Saudi based managers, coordinators and volunteers to ensure pilgrims perform their Hajj and Umrah with absolute peace of mind. The expertise also includes making the best travel arrangements customized for every family/group’s needs and affordability including the best Umrah and Hajj Services.
